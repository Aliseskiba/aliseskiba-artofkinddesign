My Work:

Vela App

A web app for surfers, divers and sailors. Vela is designed to be easy and enjoyable to use, with our #1 goal to keep people safe on the water. The apps on the market are often cluttered and overwhelming, or have a big learning curve to use. With Vela we aimed for an intuitive interface in which people can see weather, plan trips and learn in forums!

Role: UX research, UX/ UI design
